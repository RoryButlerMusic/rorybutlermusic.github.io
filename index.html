About

Rory Butler is a skilled musician and entertainer who provides entertainment for any situation with nothing more than a guitar. Having a wealth of experience on stage and in studio, the unique guitar arrangements of Rory Butler never fail to keep an audience captivated, or to provide the right mood for an occasion.

With a style that lends itself well to creating atmosphere in a restaurant, cocktail bar or at events such as weddings and corporate functions, Rorys arrngements of popular songs are great as background ambience, or as the main entertainment for an evening.

With a penchant for genres ranging from jazz to punk, pop and blues, Rory does not restrict himself to just one style, and creates solo guitar arrangements of music from all styles. Surprising many with his song choice, bringing tunes that are usually unheard of in a solo guitar format.

Music

The songs that Rory arranges vary in style greatly, the best idea of his song selection can come from his social media pages, or Youtube Channel, where regular recorded uploads are posted. With a wealth of different songs and styles available, Rory can adhere to any requirements. From modern pop, to classics, jazz and even traditional tunes, Rory has enough material to fit any mood or theme.

Each arrangement does have a hint of one of Rory's greatest influences - jazz. With lush chords and intricately played melodies, and a few improvised solos occasionally thrown in, the jazz style allows almost any song to be arranged in a way that fits the voice of the guitar beautifully. Not only is each arrangement largely inspired by the sounds of jazz music, but with a large catalogue of jazz tunes themselves, any cocktail hour or restaurant that needs an air of sophistication will be in safe hands.
